Convenient and Easy Online Booking

Experience hassle-free bus ticket booking with our online booking system. Whether you're planning a solo trip or traveling with a group, our user-friendly platform allows you to book your tickets in a few simple steps.

  • create your account first or login if alrady have an account.
  • after succseseful registration, login to your account
  • navigate to book from your dashbord
  • enter time and date
  • Enter passenger details and choose your seats
  • Proceed to secure online payment

Our online booking system provides real-time updates on seat availability and allows you to select your preferred seats. You can also customize your booking by adding additional services such as meal options or baggage handling.
